History

The Ram 1500 Trx was created in 2020, many Ram followers were very excited about the release of this new performance truck. The Trx was labled the "Raptor Killer" with it's 6.2L Supercharged V8 engine, the competition was easy to beat. In its first race against a Raptor, the Trx smoked the truck by almost an entire second.

Specifications

Pictured above is Ram's top of the line performance truck, the 2021 ram 1500 TRX. This truck's MSRP is $72,000, putting it way above the market value of a normal full size pickup.

  • Supercharged 6.2L V8 Engine
  • 708hp
  • 0-60 in 3.7 seconds

Trx vs. Lambo

Above is a video of a Ram 1500 Trx drag racing a Lamborghini Huracan. In the short drag of 1/4 of a mile, the Trx keeps up. Eventually though, the Trx is burned... because it's a Lamborghini.
