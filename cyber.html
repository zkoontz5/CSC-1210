History

Tesla is always trying to reach for the moon in everything they do, the Cybertruck was a recent creation by Elon Musk that suprised many people. The weird paradigm design of the truck was inspired by diffrent vehicles and even planes, the biggest influencer of this being fighter jets.

Pictured above is the 2022 Tesla Cybertruck, Tesla’s only non-sedan/SUV ever made. The Cybertruck’s MSRP ranges from 3 different MSRP’s, this depends on the motor you choose. There is a single motor, a dual motor, and a trio motor. The highest MSRP is the trio motor which is $69,000. Now unlike the TRX and the Raptor, this truck is fully electric.

  1. Single, dual and tri motor
  2. 0-60 in 2.7 seconds
