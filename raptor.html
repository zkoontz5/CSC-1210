History

The Ford Raptor was created in 2010, one of the first of it's kind, With a 6.2L V8, this truck was unmatched on the road for almost 10 years. Shortly after being relased the Raptor was tested in many offraod terrains. The Raptor went on to win multiple baja championships, with production and sales of the truck increasing every year.

Specifications

Pictured above is Ford's top of the line performance truck, the 2021 Ford F-150 Raptor. This truck's MSRP is $65,000, making this trucks MSRP cheaper than the MSRP of the TRX.

  • Twin Turbo, 3.5 liter V6 engine
  • 450hp
  • 0-60 in 4.9 seconds
