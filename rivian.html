History

Rivian was founded in 2010 by a MIT graduate. The first production of this truck started in the last few years, with only 150 made so far. This truck has set the standard for luxury and performance, with a quick 0-60 and a top of the line interior, this truck will be hard to compete with. A notabale mention for the Rivian company is that Amazon bought 21 billion dollars worth of stocks in the company!

Pictured above is the 2021 Rivian R1T, a new electric truck that started as a concept, but is now turning into a largley anticipated vehicle. The R1T's MSRP is $75,000, making this truck more expensive than the Cybertruck, TRX and Raptor.

  1. Dual Electric Motor
  2. 754hp
  3. 0-60 in 3 seconds
