Background

Performance trucks have been around since 1925 when Ford released the Model T Runabout. It wasn't until the middle 2000's when real performance pickup trucks were released. With the first of it's kind being the Raptor, followed by Tesla and Ram.

Phasing Out Gas Prefromance Trucks

In the recent years as enviormental laws stricten, gas performance trucks such as the Ford Raptor and the Trx have been petitioned to be phased out, Ford has even switched to a hybrid motor in the Raptor (Not fully electric, but also not fully gas)
